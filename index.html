Introduction

Balanced Bites: 10 Foods for a Healthy Heart is a cookbook that blends culinary art with health science. It addresses the critical link between nutrition and cardiovascular health, aiming to make scientific ideas practical and accessible through heart-healthy recipes.

What is Cholesterol?

Cholesterol is a waxy substance essential for bodily functions but can be harmful in excess. LDL cholesterol, often called "bad cholesterol," contributes to artery blockages, while HDL cholesterol helps remove it. Maintaining balanced cholesterol levels is vital for heart health.
