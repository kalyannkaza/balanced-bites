Dish 1: Creamy Cinnamon Oatmeal with Banana and Berries

Ingredients:

  • 100g porridge oats
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 4 tsp demerara sugar
  • 300ml skimmed milk
  • 3 sliced bananas
  • 400g strawberries (halved)
  • 150g fat-free natural yogurt

Nutrition (per serving):

  • Kcal: 266
  • Fat: 2g
  • Saturates: 1g
  • Carbs: 53g
  • Sugars: 34g
  • Fibre: 5g
  • Protein: 12g
  • Salt: 0.24g

Steps:

  1. Combine oats, cinnamon, sugar, and milk in a saucepan.
  2. Add banana slices and cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer until creamy, adjusting milk as needed.
  4. Serve with strawberries, yogurt, and a pinch of cinnamon.
