Introduction

Balanced Bites: 10 Foods for a Healthy Heart is a cookbook that blends culinary art with health science. It addresses the critical link between nutrition and cardiovascular health, aiming to make scientific ideas practical and accessible through heart-healthy recipes.

What is Cholesterol?

Cholesterol is a waxy substance essential for bodily functions but can be harmful in excess. LDL cholesterol, often called "bad cholesterol," contributes to artery blockages, while HDL cholesterol helps remove it. Maintaining balanced cholesterol levels is vital for heart health.

Cholesterol is required for the creation of vitamin D, bile acids, and hormones such as estrogen and testosterone. However, high levels of LDL cholesterol can lead to plaque buildup in arteries, increasing the risk of heart disease and stroke.

Recipes

Creamy Cinnamon Oatmeal

A heart-healthy breakfast combining oats, bananas, and strawberries. Packed with beta-glucan fiber and antioxidants.

  • Ingredients: Oats, cinnamon, demerara sugar, skimmed milk, bananas, strawberries, fat-free yogurt
  • Nutrition: 266 Kcal, 2g Fat, 53g Carbs, 5g Fiber
  1. Combine oats, milk, cinnamon, and sugar in a pot. Heat and stir until thickened.
  2. Top with bananas, strawberries, and a dollop of yogurt before serving.

Citrus-Glazed Salmon

A nutrient-packed lunch featuring omega-3 rich salmon with brown rice and a tangy lime dressing.

  • Ingredients: Brown rice, soybeans, salmon, cucumber, spring onions, lime, chili, soy sauce
  • Nutrition: 497 Kcal, 15g Fat, 61g Carbs, 34g Protein
  1. Cook rice and mix with soybeans.
  2. Prepare salmon by microwaving or grilling, and mix with lime dressing.
  3. Combine all ingredients and garnish with coriander.

Crunchy Trail Mix

An easy snack with almonds, walnuts, and dried fruit, ideal for boosting energy and reducing cholesterol.

  • Ingredients: Pumpkin seeds, almonds, sunflower kernels, walnuts, dried apricots, dark chocolate chips
  • Nutrition: 336 Kcal, 25g Fat, 22g Carbs, 11g Protein
  1. Toast nuts and seeds in a pan until aromatic.
  2. Mix with chopped apricots and chocolate chips for a delicious blend.

Golden Spiced Quinoa

A lunch option featuring quinoa, feta, and roasted almonds with warm spices like turmeric and coriander.

  • Ingredients: Quinoa, turmeric, coriander, almonds, feta cheese, parsley, lemon juice
  • Nutrition: 404 Kcal, 19g Fat, 44g Carbs, 17g Protein
  1. Cook quinoa with spices and mix with roasted almonds and crumbled feta.
  2. Drizzle with lemon juice and serve warm or cold.

A Commitment to Better Health

Maintaining low cholesterol levels is not just a dietary choice but a step toward a healthier, longer life. This cookbook is a tool for adopting heart-healthy eating habits, proving that nutritious meals can be delicious and easy to prepare.
